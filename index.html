Network Visualization Platform
Julian Bilcke, Software Engineer, ISC-PIF / CREA

Part 1: Introduction to Gephi,
or what is graph visualization?
Our world if full of complex,
subtle and massive relational data.
Companies sharing board members,
Diseases sharing symptoms,
Predators sharing preys,
People sharing friends..
We have many ecosystems,
complex technologies,
connected computers,
social networks..
there an infinite number
 of what scientists call
 Complex Systems
How to get a view
on this data?

You could simply

use a spreadsheet

and try to plot data..

But lists, tables and

matrices are limited


we need to widen our perception

In fact you might already have

the right hardware for that

Your eyes and

your brain

What you only need

are the right tools

We can model parts of

these systems into so-called

maps of "complex networks"

Introducing

Network Visualization

The art and science of

translating relational data

into understandable maps

Why is is so important?
Because maps are accessible
Because maps allow you to
see the Big Picture

And nowadays

with modern tools

we can do a lot more

Now we can create

maps from virtually

anything

Open databases, corporate data,

spreadsheets, real-time streams..

Using any scale, legend you need.

Different shapes, colors, sizes..

You can create online maps

and zoom to see

small details

Compute metrics

or

run algorithms

(if you're into that kind of stuff)

Merge networks maps

with geographical maps

if you want

Finally, you could explore

dynamic, remote,

interactive maps

In the end, we believe that

the map approach in general

can give great insights

into such complex networks

Convinced maps are awesome?
We think so too :)

Still, a few years ago

we were a bit disappointed by

existing "data mapping" tools..

They were very monolithic,

and not very friendly..

looking like spreadsheets..

:(

Made for scientists or engineers

not afraid of matrices, parameters..

ready to wait minutes

before seeing a single map..

:'(

We decided to

change this

That why
we created
Gephi
