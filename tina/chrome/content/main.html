Welcome to the 1.0alpha version of TINASoft

This software is dedicated to the mapping of electronic corpora. It is developed by the MOMA team (CREA-CNRS & Ecole Polytechnique/Institut des Systèmes Complexes de Paris Ile-de-France) within the European Project TINA (CA/STREP – CP-FP-INFSO n°245412) and supported by the New and Emerging Science and Technology programme of the seventh Framework Programme of the European Commission.

The MOMA Team focuses on the development of methods and tools for the mapping of large electronic corpora and social media.
